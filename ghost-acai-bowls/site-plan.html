Overview

Purpose

Have you ever craved food that none of the restaurants in your town serve? I feel that, and it’s why I’m here to start a brand-new Acai Bowl shop in Rexburg. Not only will we serve healthy, tasteful acai bowls but also authentic Brazilian style bowls. No more craving Acai in this town! You can have healthy, feel-good bowls, or indulge in your tasty, sweet bowls too. After all these banks, soda shops and Wendy’s built, there’s a new place to eat in town, finally!

Audience

Rexburg and surrounding cities' residents
